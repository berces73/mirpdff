Cookie Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

How MirPDF uses cookies

MirPDF uses minimal cookies. Because all file processing happens entirely in your browser, we do not need to store session data about your files on our servers.

We may use analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use our tools, helping us improve the product. These cookies do not identify you personally.

Your choices

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies will not affect the functionality of MirPDF's tools, as all processing is local.
