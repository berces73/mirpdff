Frequently Asked Questions

Will compression reduce quality?

Light compression is visually lossless. Strong compression may slightly reduce image quality in photo-heavy PDFs.

How much can a PDF be compressed?

Photo PDFs can shrink 60–70%. Text-heavy PDFs compress less (10–30%).

Can I compress then add a password?

Yes — compress first, then use our Lock PDF tool to add password protection.

Can I compress a PDF that's already been compressed?

Yes, but returns diminish quickly. A previously compressed PDF may only shrink a few percent further.

Will compression affect digital signatures?

Strong compression can invalidate embedded digital signatures. Use Light compression if signatures must remain valid.

Is there a file size limit for compression?

Free plan: up to 25 MB. Pro plan supports up to 50 MB files.

When You Need PDF Compression

Email Attachments
Gmail and Outlook both enforce a 25 MB attachment limit. Most corporate mail servers are stricter at 10 MB. A scanned contract or brochure routinely exceeds these — compression typically brings a 5 MB file to under 1 MB.
WhatsApp & Telegram File Sharing
Mobile messaging platforms cap file sizes at 100 MB but mobile networks are slow. Compressing first speeds up sending and ensures delivery.
Government & University Portals
Turkish e-government portals (e-Devlet), KYK, and university applications often enforce strict 2 MB or 5 MB upload limits. Compression is the fastest fix.
Cloud Storage Savings
Archiving years of invoices, contracts, or scanned documents? Batch-compress to reclaim 50–70% of storage space.

Pro Tips

  • Always preview the before/after file size before downloading — if savings are under 10%, the original is already well-optimised.
  • For photo-heavy PDFs (brochures, portfolios), strong compression yields 60-70% reduction. For text-heavy PDFs (contracts, reports), expect 10-30%.
  • Never compress a PDF you'll compress again — each generation of compression degrades image quality further.
  • After compressing, verify that any QR codes or barcodes remain scannable — test with your phone camera.

More Questions

Does compression affect digital signatures?

Yes — strong compression can invalidate embedded digital signatures. If the PDF has legal signatures, use light compression only.

Why is my PDF getting larger after compression?

This can happen with already-optimized PDFs. The file is already near minimum size — no further reduction is possible.
