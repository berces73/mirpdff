Frequently Asked Questions

Are Turkish characters preserved?

Yes. ç, ş, ğ, ü, ö, ı, İ characters are correctly preserved in the output.

How long does my file stay on the server?

A maximum of 1 hour after processing. Files are automatically deleted — nothing is stored permanently.

Why is formatting sometimes imperfect?

Complex graphic layouts may shift slightly — this is a limitation of all PDF-to-Word converters. Text-heavy documents convert with high accuracy.

Can I convert a scanned PDF?

Scanned PDFs need OCR first. Use our OCR PDF tool to extract text, then convert.

My converted Word file has strange characters. What happened?

This usually means the PDF used non-standard encoding. Try selecting 'Unicode' output option, or use OCR mode.

Can I convert multiple PDFs to Word at once?

Batch conversion is available on the Pro plan. Free plan processes one file at a time.

Are images in the PDF included in the Word output?

Yes. Embedded images are extracted and placed in the Word document in approximately their original positions.
