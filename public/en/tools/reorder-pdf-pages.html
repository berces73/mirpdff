Frequently Asked Questions

Can I preview pages before reordering?

Yes. Each page appears as a thumbnail so you can identify content before dragging.

Is there a page limit?

Practical limit is 100-150 pages. For longer documents, Pro account is recommended.

Can I also delete pages while reordering?

Reordering and deletion are separate tools. Use Delete PDF Pages for removal.

Is Reorder PDF Pages completely free?

Yes. This tool runs in your browser with no server cost. No registration required and no usage limits on the free plan.

Are my files private?

Browser-based tools never upload your files. For server-processed tools, files are automatically deleted within 1 hour.

What browsers support this tool?

All modern browsers are supported: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and mobile browsers on iOS and Android.

When to Reorder PDF Pages

Fixing Scan Order
Multi-page documents scanned one sheet at a time often end up out of order — especially duplex scans. Drag pages back to correct sequence in seconds.
Customising Presentation Handouts
Reorder slides for different audiences — executive summary first for management, technical detail first for engineers — from the same base PDF.
Restructuring Legal Documents
Appendices, exhibits, and sections added by different parties frequently arrive in the wrong order. Reorder before finalising for signature.
Educational Material Assembly
Combine worksheets, answer keys, and reference materials in the most pedagogically effective sequence — not the order files were created.

Pro Tips

  • Thumbnail previews let you identify page content before dragging — essential for long documents where page numbers alone are ambiguous.
  • Plan your final order before starting — map it on paper for complex reorders to avoid errors.
  • Reordering and deletion are separate tools — use Delete PDF Pages first if you need to remove pages, then reorder.
  • For very long documents (100+ pages), consider splitting into logical sections, reordering within sections, then merging back.
