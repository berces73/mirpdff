Frequently Asked Questions

Is this legally binding?

This tool creates a visual signature. For legally binding e-signatures, a qualified electronic signature (under applicable law) is required.

Can I save my signature?

Signature saving is not yet available — recreate it each session.

Can signed PDFs be modified?

Yes, technically. For tamper-evidence, use a qualified e-signature service or add password protection afterwards.

Is Sign PDF Online completely free?

Yes. This tool runs in your browser with no server cost. No registration required and no usage limits on the free plan.

Are my files private?

Browser-based tools never upload your files. For server-processed tools, files are automatically deleted within 1 hour.

What browsers support this tool?

All modern browsers are supported: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and mobile browsers on iOS and Android.

Pro Tips

  • For a more professional appearance, draw your signature rather than using a typed font — takes 10 seconds on a touchscreen.
  • Position your signature on a signature line if one exists — click and drag to place precisely.
  • For legally binding agreements under Turkish law (5070 sayılı kanun), a qualified electronic signature (nitelikli elektronik imza) is required. This tool provides a visual signature suitable for routine approvals.
  • Save your signed PDF immediately — browser sessions don't persist the signature canvas.
