Frequently Asked Questions

Can watermarks be removed?

Standard watermarks added by this tool can be removed with advanced PDF editors. For stronger protection, use password restriction too.

Can I use a logo image as a watermark?

Yes. Upload an image (PNG with transparency works best) as your watermark.

Can I add watermarks to specific pages only?

Yes. Enter a page range to apply the watermark selectively.

When to Watermark a PDF

Draft Document Control
Mark versions as DRAFT or CONFIDENTIAL before distributing for review. Prevents recipients from treating working documents as final versions.
Brand Protection
Add your company name, logo, or website URL to proprietary documents — reports, presentations, research — before external distribution.
Copyright Notice
Photographers, designers, and writers add copyright watermarks to PDF portfolios and creative work before sending to prospective clients.
Client-Specific Copies
Add the client's name as a watermark to identify which copy was distributed to which recipient — useful for tracking leaks.

Pro Tips

  • 15-25% opacity creates a visible but unobtrusive watermark that doesn't obscure the underlying text.
  • Diagonal placement (45°) is harder to crop out than corner or center placement.
  • For brand protection, combine a watermark with password restriction to prevent easy removal.
  • Test your watermark at the zoom level recipients are likely to use — what looks subtle at 100% may be prominent at 150%.
